# Digital Culture and Celebrity Phenomenon: A Comprehensive Study Course

## Course Overview
This course explores the intersection of digital culture, celebrity worship, internet memes, and online identity formation through the lens of popular culture phenomena. Using case studies and cultural analysis, students will examine how celebrities like Danny DeVito have become cultural touchstones in the digital age.

---

## Module 1: Introduction to Digital Celebrity Culture

### Learning Objectives
- Understand the evolution of celebrity culture in the digital age
- Analyze the role of social media in celebrity perception
- Examine the democratization of fame through internet platforms

### Lesson 1.1: The Digital Celebrity Ecosystem
#### Topics Covered:
- Traditional vs. digital celebrity pathways
- Platform-specific celebrity cultures (Twitter, TikTok, Reddit)
- The role of memes in celebrity status
- Case study: Danny DeVito's internet presence

#### Key Concepts:
- **Digital Celebrity**: Public figures who gain fame primarily through online platforms
- **Viral Celebrity**: Temporary fame achieved through viral content
- **Parasocial Relationships**: One-sided relationships fans develop with celebrities

### Lesson 1.2: The Anatomy of Internet Fame
#### Topics Covered:
- Virality mechanics and algorithms
- The role of fan communities in celebrity maintenance
- How traditional celebrities adapt to digital spaces
- Meme culture and celebrity iconography

#### Activities:
- Analyze trending celebrity hashtags
- Map the digital footprint of a chosen celebrity
- Create a timeline of viral celebrity moments

## Assessment Methods

### Continuous Assessment (60%)
- **Weekly Discussion Posts**: Analysis of current celebrity culture events (20%)
- **Meme Analysis Project**: Deep dive into a specific celebrity meme phenomenon (15%)
- **Digital Ethnography Report**: Observation and analysis of a celebrity fan community (15%)
- **Case Study Presentation**: Group presentation on a celebrity's digital transformation (10%)

### Final Projects (40%)
Choose one of the following:

#### Option A: Research Paper (3000-4000 words)
Original research on a specific aspect of digital celebrity culture using course frameworks and methodologies.

#### Option B: Digital Art/Meme Creation Project
Create an original digital artwork or meme series with accompanying analytical essay explaining cultural significance and creation process.

#### Option C: Documentary Film
Produce a 15-20 minute documentary exploring a specific celebrity phenomenon with academic analysis and community interviews.
